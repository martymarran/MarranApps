Data Deletion Request

Last updated: May 16, 2026

You have the right to request deletion of all personal data we hold about you. This page explains what data we store, what will be deleted, and how to submit a request.

What data we store

  • Your email address and display name (account information)
  • Your household memberships and associated expense records
  • Push notification tokens used to send you reminders
  • Usage and analytics data collected through Google Firebase Analytics

What happens when you request deletion

  • Your account and all associated profile data will be permanently deleted
  • You will be removed from any households you belong to
  • Expense records you created will be deleted; expenses shared with remaining household members may be retained for those members
  • Your push notification token will be removed
  • Analytics data retained by Google Firebase will be subject to Google's own retention and deletion policies

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

Submit a deletion request

Send an email from the address associated with your account. Include your full name (if provided) and the app you are requesting deletion for. We will verify your identity before processing the request.

Request Data Deletion

Alternatively — delete your account in-app

If you prefer, you can delete your account directly from within the app. Go to Settings → Delete Account. This will immediately delete your account and all associated data without needing to contact us.

Questions

If you have any questions about this process, please contact us at mmarran@gmail.com or review our full Privacy Policy.
