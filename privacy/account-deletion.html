Delete Your Account

Last updated: May 16, 2026

You can delete your account at any time. Deleting your account is permanent and will remove all of your personal data from our systems.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your profile information (name, email address)
  • Your household membership
  • All expense records associated with your account, if you are the sole household member
  • Your push notification token
  • Any saved biometric credentials on your device

If your household has other members, those members and their shared expense records will not be affected. Analytics data retained by Google Firebase is subject to Google's own deletion policies.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly from within the Expense Aware app:


1

Open the app and sign in to your account

2

Tap the Settings tab

3

Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account

4

Confirm the deletion when prompted — your account and data will be removed immediately

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you are unable to access the app, send an email from the address associated with your account. We will verify your identity and delete your account within 30 days.
